An open professional network for statisticians in the Netherlands.


Who we are

Young Statisticians Netherlands is an organization for students, early-career researchers and professionals who are enthusiastic about statistics. We are affiliated with the vvs-or.

What we do

Our mission is to enrich the environment in which young statisticians in the Netherlands take the first steps of their career. We aim to provide a network through which young statisticians are able to get into contact with each other, discuss statistical topics and learn from each other. In addition to connecting young statisticians, we provide opportunities to learn from and discuss with leading experts, to develop professional skills and to bring together statisticians from the many different areas of science and industry in which statistics plays a vital role.
