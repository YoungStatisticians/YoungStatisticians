An open professional network for beginning statisticians in the Netherlands.


Who we are

We are the young section of the vvs-or, intended to promote a countrywide network of people in our field.

What we do

Our mission is to bring young statisticians together. We aim to provide engaging discussions and create interdisciplinarity. To be at forefront of an information-driven society.
