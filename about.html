The Team

Here you can read about the current board of Young Statisticians. Scroll down to view all of them!

Laura Verkerk

Laura is a Student of the Statistical Science for the Life and Behavioural Sciences master's program at Leiden University. Before she studied Psychology (Bsc.) and she is working on a thesis for Methodology & Statistics in Psychology. At the moment Laura is at the CBS (Central Agency for Statistics), where she is doing an Internship on the relation between Flu, Cold and a higher mortality rate in the Netherlands, until midway January 2017. Laura enjoys applications of statistics to real-life problems and challenges. Together with Annette she represents the Young Statisticians' media team!

Kees Mulder

Kees likes posterior model probabilities, coffee, bootstrapping, music festivals and programming. As a PhD candidate at Utrecht University, Kees works on Bayesian analysis of circular data. He’s worked previously at CITO and as a researcher for CentERdata’s LISS panel. Furthermore, he’s involved in PhD representation , teaches Structural Equation Modeling, holds a BSc. in Psychology and a MSc. in statistics, and only supports null hypothesis tests that support null hypotheses. He’s been with YS since mid 2015, where he organized events such as the p-value science café.

Machiel Visser

Machiel is a student of the Statistical Science master’s programme at Leiden University, specialising in Life and Behavioural Sciences, as well as in Data Science. He is also member of the Education Committee. Before, he did a research master’s in Biology at the same university, where he worked on multiple research projects in Biogeography. In his spare time he enjoys playing tennis, skiing, hiking, and reading. Within the Young Statisticians board Machiel holds the position of Treasurer and keeps track of all finances.

Jonas Haslbeck

Jonas is a PhD student at the Psychosystems lab at the University of Amsterdam, working on multivariate time series models. Before, he studied psychology (Bsc, University of Graz, Austria) and statistics (MSc, Utrecht University) and completed research research exchanges and internships in Mexico, Germany, Luxembourg and the UK. Next to his PhD project, Jonas does research on decision making and is interested in open science/data/code and large scale data (re)analyses. At YS, he serves as the chair of the team. For more info see here.

Erik-Jan van Kesteren

Erik-Jan (GitHub) is a second-year master student at the Methodology and Statistics programme at Utrecht University. He is currently working on feature selection in high-dimensional data such as gene sequences. In addition, he has become a team member at JASP. He loves programming and other forms of being creative at the computer, such as photography and a bit of design. As the Young Statisticians’ designer, he maintains the website as well as the visual identity of the organisation.

Annette Emerenciana

Annette is a master student Statistical Science at Leiden University. Besides statistics, she has studied Bio-Pharmaceutical Sciences (BSc. and MSc., Leiden University). As a board member of the Young Statisticians, she is in charge of managing the Young Statisticians’ media channels together with Laura.
